Privacy Policy for Quick Nav Tab

Last updated: July 1, 2023

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Quick Nav Tab ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles your data when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection and Use

Quick Nav Tab is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information from our users.

Data Storage

The extension uses Chrome's storage.sync API to store your categories and shortcuts data. This data is synchronized across your devices where you are signed into Chrome with the same account. This data is stored locally on your devices and in your Google account's synchronized storage. We do not have access to this data.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Browsing history
  • IP addresses
  • Cookies
  • Search queries
  • Website content

Third-Party Services

The extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics or advertising services.

Background Images

When you upload a background image, it is stored locally using Chrome's storage API and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.
